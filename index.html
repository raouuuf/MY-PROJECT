FAQ

How does the scanning work? +
Our system uses NFC (Near Field Communication) technology. Simply tap your phone or RFID/NFC reader against your animal's tag to access their complete information and receive personalized feeding recommendations.
Do I need internet for the platform to work? +
An internet connection is recommended for real-time data syncing. However, the app supports offline mode where you can still scan tags and access cached data.
What information can I see about each animal? +
You can access health status, feeding history, nutritional requirements, weight tracking, vaccination records, and personalized recommendations for each animal.
Can it be used for large farms? +
Yes! FeedTech is designed to scale from small family farms to large commercial operations with hundreds or thousands of animals.
How accurate are the feeding recommendations? +
Our AI algorithms are trained on thousands of farms and updated continuously. Accuracy improves over time as the system learns your farm's specific conditions and preferences.
